Campus Life

School Auditorium

Kamalaya

Kamala Niketan has multiple areas for children to showcase their talent, hold guest lectures and student congregation areas. The school auditorium “Kamalaya” is a purpose designed theatre-like air-conditioned space with a seating capacity of six hundred and fifty people. The auditorium has been purpose designed with state-of-the-art lighting and acoustics to showcase stage productions, music and dance concerts, guest lectures and other functions.

school library

Library with more than 4 lakh books

The school has two libraries, one in the Montessori Primary and one in the Senior school, stocked with over 4 Lakh titles that cover fiction and non-fiction in all genres and subjects. In addition, the library contains a stock of multimedia materials for use in the classes. KNMS believes in inculcating a strong reading habit in its children. Students are actively encouraged to use the library for independent study and for research towards their presentations and projects.

HEALTH & WELL-BEING INFRASTRCUTURE

Yoga

Schools have a critical role in supporting students to make healthy lifestyle choices and to understand consequences on lifelong health and wellbeing. There is well researched evidence to show an important link between the health and wellbeing to positive academic performance. We offer a range of support services and programs to ensure your child feels a sense of belonging and receives the best possible education.

School playground

Sports

The campus boasts of a large sporting facility strengthened by a structured sports program. The school grounds and sports program provide students with an opportunity to hone their skills in Team (Cricket, Football) as well as individual sports (Badminton, Table Tennis, Skating, Archery, Karate, Taekwondo, Chess) and athletics. School sports infrastructure include basketball courts, badminton courts, football field, cricket pitch, skating ring, volleyball courts and an athletics field.

Extra-curricular activity

Dance, Drama, Singing etc...

Students visual and performing art skills are enhanced through programs weaved into their regular schedules with adequate infrastructure to support drawing, painting, craft, sculpture, music (Classical, Indian & Western), dance (Classical & Modern) and dramatics. Purpose designed physical spaces for students to explore their artistic souls include art & craft room, band room, space for dramatics and a space for dance.
